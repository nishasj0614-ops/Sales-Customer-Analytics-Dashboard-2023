# 📊 Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard – 2023

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing **Sales performance** and **Customer behavior** for the year 2023 using interactive dashboards.  
The goal is to help stakeholders quickly understand key business metrics such as **total sales, profit, quantity sold, customer growth, and top customers**.

The dashboards provide both **high-level KPIs** and **detailed insights** for better data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🛒 Sales Dashboard Highlights
The Sales Dashboard provides a clear overview of business performance:

- 📈 **Total Sales** with year-over-year comparison
- 💰 **Total Profit** and profit growth
- 📦 **Total Quantity Sold**
- 📊 **Sales & Profit by Sub-Category**
- ⏳ **Sales and Profit Trends Over Time**
- 🔺 Highest and 🔻 Lowest performing months

---

## 👥 Customer Dashboard Highlights
The Customer Dashboard focuses on customer analytics:

- 👤 **Total Customers**
- 🧾 **Total Orders**
- 💵 **Average Sales per Customer**
- 📦 **Customer Distribution by Number of Orders**
- 🏆 **Top 10 Customers by Profit**
- 📅 Monthly customer performance trends
